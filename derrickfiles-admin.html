How to Update Your Site Mixes
1. Add/edit your mixes in the Mixes panel above
2. Come here → click Download tracks.json
3. Go to Cloudflare R2 → derrickfiles-audio bucket
4. Upload the tracks.json file (overwrite the old one)
5. Done — your site loads the new mixes automatically ✅
